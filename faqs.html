Frequently Asked Questions


What is Lean in?

   Lean In Circles are small groups who meet regularly to learn and grow together,
   and they're changing lives. People are asking for more, stepping outside their
   comfort zones, and leaning in.

   Check out the LeanInCircles.org!

Why should I join a circle?

   Circles are a great place to talk about issues you are facing as a human in Computer Science.
   They are also a great way to grow your community!
   Many people in groups form very tight friendships.

How do I join a circle?

   You can either hit the "Sign Up!" tab on the top of this page or go to http://tinyurl.com/utcsleanin.


   PS: Please share this link with all your friends! Anyone can join a Lean In Circle at any time.

What is a circle moderator?

   Circle moderators are like leaders of the group, without being over controlling. A moderator might
   be the one who sets up the cirlces' Facebook group or plans the when and where of meetings. For more
   formal meetings a moderator might put together a slide deck of they want to talk about.

   Example slide decks can be found in the "Meetings" tab.

Who decides who is the moderator?

   The circle members can vote or someone can take the initiative and take charge. Moderators won't be
   assigned by the leadership unless absolutely necessary. Cirlces are designed to opperate pretty
   much autonomously.

   There is a place on the sign up form to
   indicate if you would like to be a moderator or not.

What kind of activities do y'all do?

   That is circle dependent. Some circles meet for meals or host fun events like sleep overs.
   Check out the photo gallery for some pictures from previous meetings. You can do whatever helps
   you build your community most effectively!

Can anyone join?

   ABSOLUTELY!

   There is a place on the sign up form to indicate
   if you would like to be in a single gender or co-ed circle.

How often do circles meet?

   That is really up to your circle. You can meet as often as you would like! There isn't a lot of formal
   oversight of the cirlces. We just hope you are actually meeting and enjoying the community!


   There is a place on the sign up form to indicate how
   often you are able to meet.

Are there events with all the circles, or do you only interact with your own circle?

   There will occasionally be chapter (all the circles) events. We will keep the calendar on this site up to
   date and put events up on the Facebook Page.
